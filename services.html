Traditional Asian Healing Techniques

Each treatment is designed to work with your body's natural healing ability to provide lasting relief from pain and tension.

Ashiatsu (Deep tissue massage)

What it is: Japanese finger pressure technique using palms, thumbs, and fingers along energy pathways

Duration: 60 or 90 minutes

Benefits: Relieves chronic pain, reduces stress, improves circulation, restores energy balance

Best for: Chronic tension, stress relief, general wellness

Accupressure (Trigger point therapy)

What it is: Targeted pressure applied to specific muscle knots that cause referred pain

Duration: 30, 60, or 90 minutes

Benefits: Eliminates muscle knots, reduces referred pain, improves range of motion

Best for: Specific pain points, sports injuries, headaches

Cupping therapy

What it is: Traditional Chinese technique using suction cups to release muscle tension and improve blood flow

Duration: 45 or 60 minutes

Benefits: Reduces inflammation, relieves muscle tension, promotes healing, improves circulation

Best for: Muscle soreness, inflammation, recovery from injury

Gua Sha (Scraping)

What it is: Ancient technique using smooth tools to scrape skin and release toxins and tension

Duration: 30 or 45 minutes

Benefits: Breaks up scar tissue, reduces inflammation, improves circulation, releases toxins

Best for: Chronic pain, inflammation, muscle stiffness

Assisted stretching

What it is: Therapist-guided stretching to improve flexibility and release tight muscles

Duration: 30 or 60 minutes

Benefits: Increases flexibility, prevents injury, improves posture, releases tension

Best for: Athletes, desk workers, flexibility issues

Ice therapy

What it is: Controlled cold treatment to reduce inflammation and numb pain

Duration: 15-30 minutes (add-on service)

Benefits: Reduces swelling, numbs acute pain, speeds recovery

Best for: Recent injuries, inflammation, post-workout recovery

Facials & Skincare

Experience the perfect blend of results-driven skincare and relaxation as we help you achieve healthy, radiant skin with customized treatments and homecare recommendations.

Body scrub

What it is: Full body treatment using exfoliating sugar or salt scrub followed by a nourishing moisturizer application.

Duration: 45 or 60 minutes

Benefits: Removes dead skin cells, improves skin texture, boosts circulation, promotes relaxation

Best for: Dry or rough skin, pre-event preparation, monthly maintenance

Body wrap

What it is: Therapeutic treatment where the body is wrapped in mineral-rich mud, seaweed, or hydrating masks to detoxify and nourish.

Duration: 60 or 90 minutes

Benefits: Hydrates skin, reduces bloating, improves skin tone, promotes detoxification

Best for: Dehydrated skin, special occasions, cellulite concerns, relaxation

Facial

What it is: Customized skincare treatment including cleansing, exfoliation, extractions, mask, and moisturizing tailored to your skin type.

Duration: 60 or 90 minutes

Benefits: Deep cleanses pores, improves skin texture, reduces signs of aging, promotes healthy glow

Best for: All skin types, acne concerns, anti-aging, monthly skincare maintenance

LED light treatment

What it is: Non-invasive therapy using specific wavelengths of light to stimulate cellular repair and collagen production.

Duration: 20 or 30 minutes

Benefits: Reduces inflammation, stimulates healing, minimizes fine lines, improves skin clarity

Best for: Acne treatment, anti-aging, sensitive skin, post-procedure healing

Microneedling

What it is: Minimally invasive treatment using fine needles to create controlled micro-injuries that stimulate natural collagen production.

Duration: 45 or 60 minutes

Benefits: Reduces scarring, minimizes pores, improves skin texture, enhances product absorption

Best for: Acne scars, fine lines, uneven skin texture, hyperpigmentation

Dermaplaning

What it is: Gentle exfoliation technique using a surgical blade to remove dead skin cells and fine facial hair.

Duration: 30 or 45 minutes

Benefits: Smoothes skin texture, enhances product penetration, creates flawless makeup application, immediate glow

Best for: Dull skin, fine lines, peach fuzz removal, pre-event preparation

Chemical peel

What it is: Controlled application of acid solution to remove damaged skin layers and reveal fresh, healthy skin underneath.

Duration: 30 or 45 minutes

Benefits: Reduces sun damage, minimizes fine lines, improves skin tone, accelerates cell turnover

Best for: Sun damage, hyperpigmentation, acne scars, anti-aging concerns
